About Sonya

Sonya White has built a career entertaining audiences across the country with relatable stories, clean humor, and a unique Southern perspective. Her performances blend real-life experiences, sharp observations, and hilarious crowd connection into a comedy show audiences remember.

She has appeared on HBO, Comedy Central, NBC’s Last Comic Standing, CBS, CMT’s Southern Fried Chicks, Cartoon Network, and multiple national comedy tours and festivals.

Whether performing in theaters, comedy clubs, cruise ships, or corporate events, Sonya delivers a show that feels authentic, energetic, and genuinely fun for all audiences.
